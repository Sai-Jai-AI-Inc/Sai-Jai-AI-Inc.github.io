How It Works

  1. Clients submit their AI challenge and requirements.
  2. We create custom evaluation datasets based on the client's needs.
  3. The challenge is published on our platform for vendors to compete.
  4. Developers worldwide submit their solutions and test against the evaluation set.
  5. Results are displayed on a public leaderboard, driving competition and innovation.
  6. The top-performing solution(s) are awarded the contract.

Data Protection and Privacy

  1. Compliance with International Standards: We ensure full compliance with global data protection laws, including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for the EU, PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) for Thailand, and other relevant international regulations. This guarantees that your data is handled with the utmost care and respect for privacy.
  2. Vendor Vetting and Compliance: All third-party vendors and partners we work with are thoroughly vetted to meet our strict data protection and security standards. We require all partners to adhere to GDPR, PDPA, and other applicable laws, ensuring 100% compliance throughout the process.

  4. Your Rights as a Customer: You have full control over your data. At any point, you may request access, updates, or the deletion of your data by contacting us. We are committed to transparency and ensuring you retain ownership and control of your information.
